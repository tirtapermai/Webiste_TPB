ABOUT US

PT. TIRTA PERMAI BAHARI

PT. TIRTA PERMAI BAHARI (TPB) was established to meet the dynamic and demanding market of shipping agency services across all regions of Indonesia. Our foundation was driven by the goal of not just participating, but offering superior and more efficient services for the fleet activities within our group and for external clients.

Since our inception, we have continuously improved our professionalism and expanded our operational reach, earning the trust of reputable **national and international shipping companies**.

CORE BUSINESS

Our Services

We cover every aspect of vessel operations, ensuring minimum downtime and maximum compliance across all Indonesian ports.

Port Clearance & Operations

Complete services including ship handling, operations, stowage planning, and full port clearance formalities (entry/exit, documents, authority coordination).

Crew Handling

Our team manages seamless crew transfer, including documentation, transportation, and coordination, ensuring minimal disruption to vessel operations.

Husbandary

Provides Husbandry and Owners’ Matters services designed to support the overall operational needs of the vessel.

Docking & Repair Agency

We coordinate vessel dry docking and repairs with trusted facilities, ensuring high-quality maintenance services for your fleet.

Protective Agency Services

Acting as protective agents, we safeguard the interests of vessel owners and charterers during port stays.

Bunker Fuels

We offer a range of marine support services, such as provisioning, bunkering, and technical assistance, tailored to meet vessel needs.
